Pause command

Pause or unpause (.unpause) antispam or logging for a certain duration

.pause {module} |{duration}|

.unpause {module}

{module}

Either 'antispam' or 'logging'

{duration} - optional

Any combination of the following keys (these numbers are examples), separated by a space

  • 30m - precede the letter 'm' with a number specifying minutes
  • 2h - precede the letter 'h' with a number specifying hours
  • 5d - precede the letter 'd' with a number specifying days

Examples

.pause logging

Pauses the logging module until '.unpause logging' is used

.pause antispam 4h 15m

Pauses the antispam module until '.unpause antispam' is used or 4 hours 15 minutes pass

.pause logging 1d 2d 3h 3h

Pauses the logging module until '.unpause logging' is used or 6 days 6 hours pass
