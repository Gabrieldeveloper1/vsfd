History command

View your attribute history

.history |{module}| |{member}|

{module} - optional

The attribute history module to jump to
If omitted, the homepage selector with available options will be brought up
Currently, the following options are available:

  • avatar --> View your 20 most recent profile pictures
  • username --> View your 20 most recent usernames
  • status --> View your 20 most recent custom statuses

{member} - optional

The member whose attributes you would like to view
If omitted, you will view your own attributes

Examples

.history

View the homepage (the available options) for your own attribute history

.history status

Jump right to your custom status history

.history Disguard avatar

View Disguard's avatar (profile picture) history
