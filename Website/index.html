Disguard

Standing at attention, guarding your servers

We've all been there. We all know what it's like to be raided. Avoid the pain of being raided with Disguard, a powerful bot that can automatically mute, kick, or ban people who raid your server. Don't like automation? We got that covered too. The bot can be configured to flag messages it deems as spam, and let the mods do the decision making. After all, we're not quite to the point of automation being everything. This bot also contains a full suite of moderation features such as quick commands to mute, kick, ban members, and more. Once you see the full selection of options and customization offered by Disguard, you'll most likely feel that your other moderation and logging bots aren't useful anymore. Finally, this bot is currently 100% free, so don't pay premiums for features you can get here for free! Disguard is developed by the same developer as RicoBot. The purpose of the Disguard project is to innovate on logging, moderation, antispam, and cybersecurity. Want to support the future of awesome? Check out the links at the top of the website!

Quick features overview

  • Full-fledged and automated anti-spam filters with plenty of customization
  • Keep track of the activity in your server with informative logging features, including image and attachment logging
  • View details about roles, members, or channels with one click via reactions attached to log messages
  • Get a security rating for your server or specific channels, and learn how to make your server more secure (SOON)
  • A sophisticated 'info' command gives you information about any aspect of your server at your fingertips
